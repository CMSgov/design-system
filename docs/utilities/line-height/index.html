    utilities/line-height.scss
    UsageGuidance

    In CSS, the line-height property controls the leading (led-ing). Leading refers to the vertical distance from the baseline of one line to the baseline of the next.

    Accessibility

    Many people with cognitive disabilities have trouble tracking lines of text when a block of text is single spaced. Providing spacing between 1.5 to 2 allows them to start a new line more easily once they have finished the previous one. — WCAG 2.0 Compliance Techniques
