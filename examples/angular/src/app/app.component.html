The First Amendment This is an alert inside of an accordion item.

The First Amendment provides several rights protections: to express ideas through speech and the press, to assemble or gather with a group to protest or for other reasons, and to ask the government to fix problems. It also protects the right to religious beliefs and practices. It prevents the government from creating or favoring a religion.

The Third Amendment This is an alert inside of an accordion item that is warning you.

The Third Amendment prevents government from forcing homeowners to allow soldiers to use their homes. Before the Revolutionary War, laws gave British soldiers the right to take over private homes.

The Fourth Amendment This is an alert inside of an accordion item that informing you of an error.

The Fourth Amendment bars the government from unreasonable search and seizure of an individual or their private property.
